## Simple Cheese Pizza

**Ingredients:**

* 1 ball of pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
* 1/2 cup pizza sauce
* 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
* Olive oil (for brushing)
* Dried oregano (optional)

**Instructions:**

1. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit (230 degrees Celsius). 
2. Lightly flour a surface and roll out the pizza dough into a 12-inch circle.
3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
4. Brush the dough lightly with olive oil.
5. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
7. If desired, sprinkle dried oregano over the cheese.
8. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
9. Let the pizza cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your homemade cheese pizza!
