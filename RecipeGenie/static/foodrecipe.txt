## Simple Cheese Pizza

**Ingredients:**

* 1 ball of pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
* 1 cup pizza sauce
* 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

**Instructions:**

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
2. Lightly flour a baking sheet.
3. Place the pizza dough on the baking sheet and use your fingers to spread it out into a circle shape, leaving a slight edge for the crust. 
4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edge. 
5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. 
6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. 
7. Let the pizza cool slightly before slicing and serving.

**Optional Toppings:**

* Pepperoni
* Sausage
* Mushrooms
* Onions
* Green peppers
* Black olives

**Tips:**

* For a crispy crust, use a pizza stone or baking sheet.
* Don't overcrowd the pizza with toppings.
* If you like a thicker crust, use more dough.
* Feel free to experiment with different cheeses and toppings.
